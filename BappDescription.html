This Burp Extension lets you generate AWS Curl commands or cURL with SigV4 from an API. With a simple right-click, you can bring up the extension context menu, select the extension and instantly the generated command will be copied to your clipboard.

Features

The extension provides two primary features:

  • Generate AWS Curl commands for a given API.
  • Generate cURL commands with SigV4 for a given API.

Usage

The use of the extension is pretty straightforward. Right-click to bring up the extension context menu and choose the extension. Your AWS Curl or cURL with SigV4 command is then created and automatically copied to your clipboard. The general structure of these commands can be seen below:


awscurl --service <service> --region us-east-1 \\
    -X GET \\
    -H Host: <host> \\
    -H Accept-Encoding: gzip, deflate, br \\
    -H User-Agent: <User-Agent> \\
    -H X-Amz-Date: <date> \\
    -H X-Amz-Security-Token: <Token> \\
    -d {<data>} \\
    <Endpoint-URL>


curl https://endpoint \\
    --user $AWS_ACCESS_KEY_ID:$AWS_SECRET_ACCESS_KEY \\
    -H x-amz-security-token: $AWS_SESSION_TOKEN \\
    --aws-sigv4 aws:amz:<region>:<service> \\
    -H User-Agent: <User-Agent> \\
    -H Accept: application/json, \\
    -H Accept-Language: en-US,en;q=0.5 \\
    -H Accept-Encoding: gzip, deflate, br \\
    -H Content-Type: application/json \\
    -H Origin: * \\
    -H Content-Length: 457 \\
    --data {<data>}
